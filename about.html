ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Isaac, the creator of the project AAATUM. I’m a game developer and artist working across programming, game design, illustration, and 2D art.

What is AAATUM?

I have been working as an independent game developer since 2021, creating original projects through solo work, collaborations, and game jams. In 2025, I decided to consolidate this practice under AAATUM, a personal project focused on designing short but impactful games.
